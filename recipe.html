Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix the flour and baking soda together.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and salt until it's light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the vanilla extract and eggs one at a time until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden but the centers are still soft.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.

Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies!



Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside
  2. In a pan, sauté the diced pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese Season with black pepper.
  4. While the pasta is still hot, quickly toss it with the crispy pancetta and the egg-cheese mixture until well coated. The heat from the pasta will cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and more black pepper if needed.
  6. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  7. Serve immediately, and enjoy your classic Spaghetti Carbonara!
